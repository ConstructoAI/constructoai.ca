TL;DR

Choose Procore if you run a large general contractor (100+ employees, $50M+ revenue) with complex commercial projects and a dedicated IT and admin team. Choose Constructo AI if you run a small to mid-size construction or fabrication shop (1-50 employees) and want an all-in-one ERP at a predictable flat monthly price with 57 AI advisors built in, PDF takeoff included, and no per-user fees ever.

The price difference is the elephant in the room

Procore does not publish public pricing. According to analyst reports and customer disclosures, Procore base tier starts around $375 USD per user per month and scales to $500 to $1500 per user per month for tiers that include the modules small contractors actually need (estimating, bid management, financial tools).

Constructo AI is $79.99 CAD flat per month for the entire company. Unlimited employees. No per-seat fees. No setup fees. No "talk to sales" pricing gymnastics.

For a 10-person contractor running construction or a fabrication shop, the math looks like this:

  • Procore: 10 users × $400/user/month = $4,000 USD/month = $48,000 USD per year
  • Constructo AI: $79.99 CAD/month flat = $960 CAD per year (about $700 USD)

That is a 50x to 70x price difference. The question is not "which is better" in absolute terms — it is "what do I actually need to run my business this month?"

Honest scenarios: when to pick which

Constructo AI wins

You run a small or mid-size contractor (1-50 employees)

If you are an electrical contractor, plumber, framer, drywall company, roofing crew, foundation specialist, fabrication shop, or general contractor with up to 50 people, you do not need Procore's enterprise feature set. You need to send bids fast, track time, bill clients, and stop drowning in admin. Constructo AI gives you all of that for less than $80 CAD per month, period.

Constructo AI wins

You serve customers in both English and French

If you operate in Quebec, New Brunswick, eastern Ontario, Manitoba, or near the Quebec border in the US (Vermont, New York, New Hampshire, Maine), you need bilingual quotes, invoices, and a UI your foremen can actually read. Constructo AI is natively bilingual with instant toggle. Procore is English-first with limited French support.

Constructo AI wins

You want AI that actually helps you do estimating

57 AI advisors trained on construction codes, materials pricing, methods, and standards (CSA, ASHRAE, NEC, IPC, NRCA, CWB). Ask "what's the price for installing 1500 sqft of level 4 drywall in commercial space?" and get a detailed answer in seconds. Procore's AI features are more recent and narrower in scope.

Procore wins

You are a large GC (100+ employees, $50M+ annual revenue) running complex commercial projects

Procore was built for large general contractors managing $100M+ commercial builds with dozens of subs, hundreds of RFIs per project, and corporate IT requirements. The depth of features for portfolio-level project management, resource leveling across 500+ users, and the partner marketplace are unmatched. If your business looks like this, Procore is probably the right tool.

Procore wins

You need 400+ pre-built integrations with niche construction tools

If your workflow depends on integrating with BIM 360, Autodesk Construction Cloud, Bluebeam, PlanGrid, Sage 300 CRE Enterprise, dozens of accounting and HR systems, Procore's marketplace covers practically everything in the industry. Constructo AI focuses on a smaller set of core integrations (QuickBooks, Sage 50, Stripe, SMTP).

The real question: do you need a Mercedes or a Toyota?

Procore is a Mercedes. It is a luxurious, powerful, and proven platform that does what it does extremely well. If you can afford the monthly payment and you actually use the features that justify the price, it is a great choice.

Constructo AI is a Toyota Hilux. It is reliable, gets the job done, costs a fraction of what the luxury option costs, and has every feature a contractor running 1-50 people actually uses every day. Plus a few things Procore does not have at all: 57 AI advisors, AI photo analysis, native bilingual UI, free SEAOP public tender platform, Canadian and US payroll built in.

The honest truth: most contractors below $20M in annual revenue do not need Procore. They need a tool that does estimating, billing, project tracking, time clock, and reports without bankrupting them. That is what Constructo AI is built for.
